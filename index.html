1. About Me

Hi, I’m Charlie Doubet — a senior at The University of Iowa, majoring in Business Analytics and Information Systems with a minor in Communication Studies. I’m originally from Denver, Colorado, where I’ve lived my whole life. One question I always get is, “Why did you choose Iowa over Colorado?” The short answer: my dad went here, so I grew up a proud Hawkeye. Choosing Iowa has been one of the best decisions I’ve made, giving me not only a strong education but also lifelong friendships and unforgettable experiences. One of the highlights of my college journey was studying abroad in Barcelona last spring. It was truly a life-changing experience, and something I’d recommend to everyone.
